# Requirements Discovery Interview Protocol

Use this protocol when the request needs structured elicitation.

## Question Shape
- Ask one question at a time.
- State why the answer matters in one sentence.
- Provide recommended options when useful.
- Record the decision, assumption, or open question before moving on.

## Priority Order
1. Goal and success signal.
2. Target actor or user.
3. Scope and non-goals.
4. Domain rules and terms.
5. Edge cases and failure behavior.
6. Data, permissions, privacy, and external systems.
7. Validation and launch expectations.

## Stop Conditions
Stop interviewing when:
- acceptance criteria are stable enough for a requirements brief,
- remaining unknowns can be marked as assumptions,
- the user asks to stop,
- the next step is clearly `plan-requirements-brief`, `plan-short-term-docs`, or implementation.
